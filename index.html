Everything Included

All the Tools You Need
to Fill Your Calendar

Every feature you need to take bookings, get paid, and keep customers coming back, all in one platform.

🎨
Professional Booking Templates

Choose from unlimited beautifully designed booking page templates. Customise colours, logo, and layout to match your brand. No design skills needed.

💳
Multiple Payment Methods

Accept payments via Stripe (including Google Pay, Apple Pay, Klarna & more), PayPal, Square, Mollie, and Authorize.net, all collected at the point of booking with deposit options too.

🔔
Unlimited Email Notifications

Send unlimited automated emails: confirmations, reminders, follow-ups, and custom scheduled notifications. Use your own SMTP to send from your own address.

📆
Calendar Sync

Every booking syncs automatically with Google Calendar, Outlook, or Apple Calendar. Each employee's calendar stays in perfect sync with no double bookings, ever.

💰
Employee Commissions

Set a commission percentage per employee and let the platform handle the maths. Perfect for freelancer-style setups where staff pay you a cut to use your booking system, creating a built-in revenue stream on top of your own services.

👥
Full Team & Role Management

Assign custom roles and permissions, manage employee days off, special schedules, and individual pricing per staff member. Each employee gets their own profile and self-management access.

📊
Analytics & Business Insights

Visual charts for revenue, occupancy, and performance. Track stats per employee, see your top services, and make data-driven decisions, all from your dashboard.

🧾
Invoicing & Finance Tools

Automatically generate branded invoices, configure tax rates, handle refunds, and get a full transactions overview. Gift cards and deposit payments are also built in.

🏷️
Coupons & Promotions

Create discount codes and special offers directly in your booking system. Run promotions, reward loyal customers, and drive more bookings whenever you need a boost.

🌍
Multilingual & Time Zone Support

Your booking page automatically adapts to your customer's language and local time zone, wherever they are in the world. No confusion, no missed appointments.

🔌
Zapier, Webhooks & API

Connect to thousands of apps via Zapier, send data via Webhooks, or build custom integrations using the API. Plus native connections with Mailchimp, Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, and more.

🔗
Embed, Share & Go

Embed your booking form on any website, share a standalone booking link, or add a QR code to print materials. Works with WordPress too, putting your booking page everywhere your customers are.
